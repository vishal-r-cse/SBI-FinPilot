# SBI FinPilot

## 🚀 Agentic AI Financial Life Companion

**SBI FinPilot** is an Agentic AI-powered financial companion built for the **SBI Hackathon @ GFF 2026** under the **Digital Engagement** problem statement.

It transforms digital banking from reactive to proactive by providing personalized financial guidance, intelligent recommendations, and proactive customer engagement.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional banking applications are reactive and provide limited personalized financial guidance. Customers often miss savings opportunities, investment options, and important financial alerts.

---

## Solution

SBI FinPilot leverages Agentic AI to:

* 🤖 Analyze customer financial behavior
* 🎯 Create personalized financial plans
* 💡 Recommend suitable SBI products
* 🔔 Send proactive financial alerts
* 📊 Track financial health
* 📈 Improve customer engagement
